# Spelt flour Banana bread

Created: Sep 20, 2020 12:11 PM

## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3 bananas + 1 for dem lookz
- ⅓ cup butter
- 2 cups spelt flour
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- 1 table spoon rice syrup
- 1 baking soda
- 1 pinch of salt

## How to

- bake for ±55 minutes on 180°C
